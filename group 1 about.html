About ODA SBS 

      Oromia development Association (ODA) is local, membership-based, non-governmetal organization
      established in 1993 to improve the lives of people in Oromia region through sustainable integrated
      community-based programs. ODA primarily focuses on livelihood diversification, community empowerment,
      public health promotion, education, provision of potable water, environment protection, and promoting
      voluntrarism to contribute to the development of the region.

Vision

       futire leaders, professional who serve humanity in a diverse national and global society.

Mission 

Provide high quality education nurture talent and innovation to develop moral and spritual
               well being of each student in preparation for a life of service.
